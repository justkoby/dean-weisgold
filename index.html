Story about
Weisgold.

Effective representation, in Dean Weisgold's opinion, goes beyond the business agreement and into the community. He has handled hundreds of pro bono cases through the Philadelphia Volunteers for the Indigent Program (VIP), giving his time and knowledge to make sure that everyone, regardless of financial situation, may access justice.

Although Dean received the VIP Champion Award and the William J. Brennan Award for his decades of devotion, he believes that the true pleasure lies in the client's success. This dedication is the cornerstone of his work, not an independent endeavor.
