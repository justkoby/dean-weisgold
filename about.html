About Us

History

A Legacy of Integrity,
Service, and Excellence

Founded by Dean E. Weisgold, Esq., Weisgold Law is the result of over three decades of dedication to justice, advocacy, and the highest standards of professional conduct.

Dean began his legal journey at the University of Pennsylvania School of Law, earning his Juris Doctorate in 1988 after graduating cum laude and as a Benjamin Franklin Scholar from the same institution. Early in his career, Dean gained extensive experience working with respected law offices in Philadelphia, where he built a reputation for thorough preparation, persuasive advocacy, and unwavering integrity.

Building
Weisgold Law

After years of representing clients in both large and medium-sized firms, Dean established Dean E. Weisgold, P.C. to create a practice where clients could receive personalized, high-quality representation without sacrificing accessibility or compassion.

From its office in Philadelphia's Market Street District, Weisgold Law has served individuals, businesses, and communities across Eastern Pennsylvania and Southern New Jersey, handling a diverse range of civil and commercial disputes.

Over time, the firm has earned recognition for its expertise in:

  • Civil and commercial litigation
  • Construction and real estate disputes
  • Representation of condominium and homeowners associations

Each case is approached with a commitment to preparation, precision, and professional ethics, the principles that define Dean's practice.

Pro Bono Work

Dean handled hundreds of pro bono cases through the Philadelphia Volunteers for the Indigent Program in Philadelphia and was awarded the prestigious William J. Brennan Award in 2016 for work on income-related cases at the organization's 35th Anniversary Gala.
