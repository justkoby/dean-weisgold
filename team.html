Lead Attorney

Dean E. Weisgold

Dean E. Weisgold has been providing strategic legal counsel and aggressive representation to clients for over 30 years. His practice is built on a foundation of professional precision and direct, honest communication.

Licensed in Pennsylvania and New Jersey, Dean handles complex civil and commercial litigation, real estate matters, and representation for community associations. He is known for his ability to resolve intricate legal disputes through both strategic negotiation and courtroom advocacy.
